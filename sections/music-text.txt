ABOUT RADECKT

A rising force in the underground house scene, Radeckt blends melodic house, indie dance and techno into hypnotic, club-driven records played worldwide.

His releases span key labels including Human by Default (Bedouin), Marginalia (Eli & Fur), AZZUR, Renaissance, Out of Options and Ultra, with standout tracks like Otun, Radiate (Remix) and Take Down.
